Hear the story about 'Maliteboho Fried Chicken

'Maliteboho once had very big dreams when she was still young, but life became harder than she expected.
Many opportunities passed her, and she felt broken inside. There was a time she believed she would never succeed in life.
One afternoon, while scrolling on Facebook, she found a short story about a powerful woman.
That woman came from a poor and disadvantaged background, but she never gave up. She kept believing, kept working,
and today she employs both men and women. 'Maliteboho felt hope rise again in her heart. She started small with one stove,
courage, and a dream. Today her kitchen feeds many people in Maseru with love and hard work.

To every disadvantaged person out there: your beginning does not decide your ending.
